Protein is the core class of our ontology. 7 subclasses arise from this: Structural, Storage, Transport, Catalytic, Regulatory, Signaling/receptor and Immunological/infectious.
Protein class has the following relationships with other classes:
	Protein hasLength LengthProperty
	Protein isFromSpecies Species // Species hasProtein Protein
	Protein isCodedByGene Gene // Gene codifiesProtein Protein
	Protein isSameProteinAs Protein
	Protein isInLocation CellularLocation // CellularLocation hasProteinLocated Protein
	Protein isInvolvedIn Process // Process hasProteinInvolved Protein
	Protein hasFunction Function // Function isHadByProtein Protein
